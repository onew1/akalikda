K/DA AKALI OVERVIEW

AKALI

아칼리

(voiced by Soyeon) is an assassin-type character in League who weaves in and out of the darkness and throws kunai at opponents. In K/DA, Akali is the rapper of the group. Soyeon said she tried to "feel like Akali and move as if I were Akali" during the motion capture for Pop/Stars.
